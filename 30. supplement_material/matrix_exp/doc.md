### 矩阵指数的思考
$$
    M = S\Sigma S^{-1}
    \\
    \sum^{\infin}_{n=0}\cfrac{M^n}{n!} = S[\sum^{\infin}_{n=0}\cfrac{\Sigma^n}{n!}]S^{-1}
    \\
    e^{M} =  \sum^{\infin}_{n=0}\cfrac{M^n}{n!} 

$$
